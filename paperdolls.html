Paper Dolls

2024 - 2025

Welcome to the Dream Chasers collection, a thoughtfully designed mix-and-match series of paper dolls that spark imagination, creativity, storytelling, and playful learning. This digital DIY kit encourages hands-on exploration of outfits, character creation, and layering logic, while also reinforcing left and right orientation and attention to detail through interactive play.

The system is built for expansion. All dolls and outfits across the Dream Chasers collection are fully compatible - children can mix styles freely, build stories across sets, and grow their collection over time.

Each kit includes a full set in color and black-and-white for easy printing, base dolls across six skin tones, and one black-and-white base doll for full customization - face, hair, skin tone, all theirs to decide. Hand-drawn in Affinity Designer. The project grew out of my Master's in Child Culture Design - and out of losing my mom. The aim is always to keep it as unisex and open-ended as possible - as much as the market leaves room for.
