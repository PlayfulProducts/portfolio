Durance - TR

2007 - 2015

Brand adaptation • luxury home fragrance • retail & packaging • Turkish market

Led complete brand adaptation of French luxury home fragrance retailer Durance for Turkish market entry. Managed visual identity across all flagship stores in multiple cities, packaging, and marketing materials while preserving the brand's Provençal heritage and premium positioning.

Store Launch Campaign

The market entry called for an event as considered as the brand itself. Scented invitation cards embedded with fragrance samples were produced for the VIP launch, creating a first touchpoint that was both tactile and true to what Durance is about. The event brought together 500+ guests for an interactive brand experience built around fragrance matching in-store, turning product discovery into a designed moment rather than a sales floor encounter.

Retail & Marketing

The scope of the adaptation covered every customer-facing surface. Over 200 product packaging items were localised for the Turkish market, with Turkish and English language versions produced across the full catalog and brochure range. Seasonal promotional materials, point-of-sale displays, window graphics, gift packaging, and shopping bags were all produced to specification and rolled out across retail locations. Each piece maintained the refined visual language of the parent brand while functioning in a new market context.
